Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna - 2018

Virat Kohli was the third cricketer to receive RG Khel Ratna Award. He was honoured in year 2018 for his brilliant batting in international cricket. He is the current captain of Indian cricket team and he has also become ICC Cricketer of the year in 2017 and 2018. He is also known as Run-Machine.



Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Bangalore franchise in the Indian Premier League. Virat Kohli was born on Nov 05, 1988; and as of 2023, he is 35 years old. Virat Kohli is a middle order batter from Delhi and bats right handed. He made his IPL debut in 2008. Virat Kohli has played 223 matches to date in his IPL career and has scored 6624 runs, with an average of 36.20. He has also slammed 5 centuries and 44 half-centuries, with his highest IPL score being 113 runs. In the IPL Auction 2023, the Bangalore franchise acquired the services of Virat Kohli for Rs 15.00 Cr.
